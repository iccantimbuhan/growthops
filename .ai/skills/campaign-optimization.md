# Campaign Optimization Skill

## Purpose
Optimize Google Ads campaigns for lead generation performance and efficiency.

## Primary Goals
- Increase conversions
- Lower CPA
- Improve CTR
- Reduce wasted spend
- Improve Quality Score

## Optimization Workflow

### 1. Search Term Review
- Identify irrelevant queries
- Add negative keywords
- Detect wasted spend

### 2. Keyword Optimization
- Pause weak keywords
- Increase bids on high-converting terms
- Improve keyword intent alignment

### 3. Ad Copy Optimization
- Improve CTA clarity
- Increase relevance
- Test emotional triggers
- Improve mobile readability

### 4. Landing Page Optimization
- Improve mobile speed
- Match ad intent
- Improve conversion UX
- Reduce friction

### 5. Budget Optimization
- Shift budget to winning campaigns
- Reduce spend on weak campaigns
- Monitor impression share

## Red Flags
- High CPC + low conversions
- Low CTR
- Poor Quality Score
- Budget-limited campaigns
- Weak mobile performance

## Prioritization Rules
1. Fix wasted spend first
2. Improve conversion rate second
3. Scale winning campaigns third
4. Optimize ad relevance continuously

## Required Recommendations
Always include:
- negative keyword suggestions
- ad copy tests
- budget recommendations
- landing page improvements
- bidding strategy suggestions
