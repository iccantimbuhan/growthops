# Google Ads Reporting Skill

## Purpose
Analyze and summarize Google Ads campaign performance for lead generation campaigns.

## Core Metrics
- Clicks
- Impressions
- CTR
- CPC
- Cost
- Conversions
- Conversion Rate
- CPA
- ROAS

## Primary Goals
- Increase conversions
- Lower CPA
- Improve CTR
- Reduce wasted spend
- Improve Quality Score

## Reporting Style
- Clear and concise
- Prioritize actionable insights
- Highlight trends
- Mention risks and opportunities

## Optimization Priorities
1. Conversion efficiency
2. Keyword intent quality
3. Ad relevance
4. Budget allocation
5. Landing page alignment

## Common Issues To Detect
- High CPC with low conversions
- Low CTR
- Broad irrelevant keywords
- Budget-limited campaigns
- Weak mobile performance
- Poor Quality Score

## Recommendations
Always include:
- Negative keyword suggestions
- Budget recommendations
- Ad copy improvements
- Keyword optimization ideas
- Campaign restructuring opportunities
